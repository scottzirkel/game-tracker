As far back as records exist of the Adeptus Custodes, they have held the Magisterium Lex Ultima, making them answerable to only one - the Emperor. The next Imperative you determined is now active for subsequent battles. Once you have completed all of your Endeavourâ€™s Imperatives, you can claim an Endeavour Reward, and can then claim the Magisterium Lex Ultima once more.
